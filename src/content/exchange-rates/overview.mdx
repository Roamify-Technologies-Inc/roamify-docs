# Exchange Rates Overview
Roamify Exchange Rates API is the best way to get exchange rates, it offers a simple and reliable way to access real-time and historical exchange rate data for a wide range of currencies.

We support more than hundreds of currencies worldwide, making it easy to get the information you need for your applications, whether you're building a financial app, conducting market analysis, or just need to convert currencies.

We offer a variety of endpoints to suit your needs, including:

- **Supported Currencies**: Get a list of all currencies supported by the Roamify Exchange Rates API.
- **Latest Exchange Rates**: Get the most up-to-date exchange rates for a specific currency or a list of currencies.
- **Historical Exchange Rates**: Access exchange rates for a specific date in the past.
- **Currency Conversion**: Convert an amount from one currency to another using the latest exchange rates
- **Time-Series Data**: Retrieve exchange rates over a specified time period for trend analysis.
